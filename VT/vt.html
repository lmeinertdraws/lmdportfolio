How You Can Help Vietnamese Ethnic minorities
(and drink delicious tea)

Scattered across Vietnam, are many different ethnic groups with unique lifestyles and cultures. Unfortunately, the pressures of modernity and industrial agriculture, threaten their way of life. Viet Tea is partnered with Evergreen Labs, an international organization dedicated to fighting industrial agriculture by supporting small farmers. Learn more about Vietnam's ethnic groups

Enjoy unique teas

We source our tea from ethnic groups and farmers, that adhere to traditional and sustainable farming methods. Many of our teas are handpicked from wild growing trees.

Support small-scale, organic agriculture

In order to combat the pressures of industrial agriculture, we source our teas from farmers who care about the health of the consumer and environment.
